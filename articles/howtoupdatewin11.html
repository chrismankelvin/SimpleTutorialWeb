    • How To Update Windows 11


    1. First, click on start button from the taskbar.




    2. Locate Settings from your Start menu. You can locate it from anywhere you want




    3. Select "Windows Update" from the left menu




    4. Click on "Windows Update"




    Now after clicking on the update button, Windows will automatically start to update itself, You maybe ask to click on Install after Windows download update. After everything restart your computer to apply all update cleanly. If you have any concerns click here to send your problems.
